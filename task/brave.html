SNOWDROP





In the snowy landscape of 1987 South Korea, Eun Young-ro, a spirited college student, finds an injured Lim Soo-ho and hides him in her dorm, believing he's a student protester. As they spend time together, a forbidden love blossoms between them, unaware that Soo-ho is a North Korean spy. Their tender moments are shadowed by political turmoil and secrets. When Soo-ho's true identity is revealed, their love faces impossible odds. Despite their deep bond, duty and fate tear them apart, leaving a lingering ache of what could have been.
